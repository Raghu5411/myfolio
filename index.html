Farm Care

Project

Farm Care is a helpful website for farmers. It uses special technology to look at pictures of plants and find out if they're sick. If a plant has a problem, Farm Care can tell the farmer quickly. This helps farmers take action fast to keep their plants healthy. The website is easy to use, so farmers don't need to be experts in computers. They just upload pictures of their plants, and Farm Care does the rest. With Farm Care, farmers can catch plant diseases early, which means less chance of losing crops. This helps farmers grow more food and makes sure we all have enough to eat. Farm Care is like having a friend in the field, helping farmers keep their plants happy and strong. It's a simple tool that makes a big difference in farming, helping farmers protect their crops and our food supply.
